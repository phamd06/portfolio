## Updated Firmware of Mesh Node Router System
I had recently replaced my old network system at home with a brand new three mesh node wifi network. Recently, I had been notified that the firmware of the router devices needed an update for security reasons. By updating the firmware of the devices, I have not only lowered the possibility of a security breach and
secured protection against vulnerabilities., I have also lowered the possibility of a bug disrupting the wifi connection which could lead to non cyber issues.
